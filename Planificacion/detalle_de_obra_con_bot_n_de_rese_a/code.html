Act I Scene I: The Prophecy

Act II Scene II: The Chamber

Synopsis

William Shakespeare’s classic tragedy of ambition and betrayal is brought to life in this visceral new production. Set against the backdrop of a decaying empire, Macbeth follows the harrowing journey of a decorated general who, spurred by prophecy and his wife's ruthless ambition, murders his way to the Scottish throne.

As the cost of his crimes begins to mount, Macbeth descends into a nightmare of paranoia and madness. This production explores the psychological toll of power and the supernatural forces that lurk in the shadows of the human heart.
